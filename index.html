Latest Work

Odyssey

I designed and implemented the logo, UI and website for Odyssey, making use of the Procursus bootstrap and libhooker, an all new hooking library designed with modern firmwares in mind.

TalkBoard 

This is a podcast organised and hosted by me and Kritanta about jailbreaking and other tech related subjects. Available on Apple and Spotify. Official Patreon and Discord links.

FlashBack

I assisted Micah (the main developer of FlashBack) with a visual overhaul of FlashBack with some upgrades to functionality too. The redesigned FlashBack app now also contains a handy guide on how to use the app.

JeffBot

This bot is very convenient for me, as it allows me to reach over 1,000 people and tell them which of the latest Apple software betas have been released with just a short message.

Friends' Projects

Below is a small collection of stuff that I think is cool, made by some cool people I know!

TuneTrack
TuneTrack is a great new app by my friend Aryan, and it's a great way to track your music habits and listening history, no matter if you use Apple Music, Spotify or Last.fm, with iOS 14 widget support too! Get it here!

Silica
Silica is a static repo compile" for jailbroken iOS devices developed by Shuga, designed to make it as easy as possible to make a personal repo that plays nicely with both Cydia and Sileo. Check it out!
